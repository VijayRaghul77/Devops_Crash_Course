  - Service type is {{ .Values.service.type }} (no NodePort/LoadBalancer/Ingress).
  - A NetworkPolicy restricts ingress to pods matched by

    (plus anything you added under .Values.networkPolicy.egress.allowTo).

    wget -qO- http://{{ include "private-nginx.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local

  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "private-nginx.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }}
